Let's Get Cooking

One of my goals this quarantine has been to learn how to be a better cook, so here are my favourite dishes I have discovered during lockdown.

Pasta Salad

This is a simple, delicious, and pretty meal - what more do you want?

Ingredients

For 2 servings:

  • 200g pasta of choice
  • 2 eggs
  • 30g rocket
  • 120g cherry tomatoes
  • 4tbsp green pesto
Instructions
  1. Boil the pasta and eggs.
    • Eggs for minimum 8 minutes.
    • Pasta for time stated on packet.
  2. Whilst boiling, cut the cherry tomatoes in half.
  3. Drain the pasta, and mix with pesto, rocket, and tomatoes.
  4. Peal the egg, cut into segments, and place on top.

Homemade Pizza

Who doesn't love a tasty pizza?

Ingredients

The base:

  • 300g bread flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 100ml warm water

The sauce:

  • 100g tomato passata
  • Fresh basil
  • 1 crushed garlic clove

For Toppings:

  • 150g mozzarella
  • 1tbsp parmesan
  • 60g halved cherry tomatoes
  • Some more fresh basil
Instructions
  1. Heat oven to 240C, or 220 for fan ovens
  2. Make the dough
    1. Stir the flour, salt and yeast into a bowl
    2. Pour in the olive oil and warm water
    3. Knead the dough for 5 minutes, then place to side covered in a tea towel
  3. For the sauce, mix all the sauce ingredients together
  4. Roll the dough out thin to ~25cm across
  5. Spread sauce over the bases, and scatter the cheese and cherry tomatoes
  6. Bake for 8-10 mins, until the bases are crispy
  7. Sprinkle on the basil, and serve

Halloumi Wrap

A quick vegetarian wrap anyone can make!

Ingredients

For each wrap:

  • 1 wrap
  • 15g rocket
  • 100g halloumi, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/3 carrot, julienned
  • tbsp hummus
Instructions
  1. Grill the halloumi for 5 minutes per side, or until golden brown
  2. Spread the hummus across the central diameter of the wrap
  3. Place the halloumi, rocket tomato, and carrot on the hummus
  4. Fold wrap at the bottom, then the sides of the wrap to encase the filling
